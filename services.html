Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Simple designs take 3-7 days, websites take 1-4 weeks, and complex systems can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes! We include multiple revisions in all our packages. The number varies by package - Starter includes 2 revisions, Professional includes 5, and Enterprise includes unlimited revisions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa), and cash payments. For larger projects, we offer flexible payment plans with 50% upfront and 50% upon completion.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Absolutely! We provide free support for 3-6 months depending on your package. This includes minor updates, bug fixes, and technical assistance. Extended support plans are also available.

Can you work with my existing brand guidelines?

Yes, we can work with your existing brand guidelines and assets. We'll ensure all new designs maintain consistency with your established brand identity while adding fresh, creative elements.
